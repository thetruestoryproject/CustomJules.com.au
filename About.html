Jules Bisschoff

I am a Prop Maker with four years of experence in the industry.
I did my BFA at the National Instute of Dramatic Art (NIDA) and graduated in 2022.
I have experence working in many diferent areas across the theatre industry from the creative aspects of building the set pieces and props to the technical and management side.

This industry is a intense yet beutifull place to be with nothing quite like it.
Without changing what makes it special, I aim to push it to its limits while exploring new areas where it can go, such as live cinema and incorprating special effects and trickery into certain set designs.

I am a well rounded problem solver with many interesting skills.
