Hero Suit

As A team we were given a task to design and build a hero suit to understand realistic presures and timelines of the industry.
We were given a ten week timeline and a $200 budget between the class. presenting several design ideas we ended up with a sleek hero suit that was still dynamic in its movemnts, in between a hero piece and a stunt suit.
With the design sorted we split up in groups where we managed our individual element, and helped on others.
I managed the wepons belt which was made out of leather, styled after a modified western gun belt.
I also assisted with the lasercutting of the EVA foam padding, the building of the light sword, assisted with making the boots and sewing of the backpack harnes.
