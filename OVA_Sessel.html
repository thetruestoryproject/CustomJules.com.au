The original chair designed by Christian Dorn.
❮ ❯

This is a replica of a OVA Sessel designed by Christian Dorn. It Was Made during my furniture making class at NIDA, where we were asked to replicate a dificult piece of furniture within a budget. We only had a $300 AUD budget which I was able to stay well below. This is mainly as labour costs were not included and as it is a theatre prop it is not a soft as the real chair.
