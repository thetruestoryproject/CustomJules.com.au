Halle Fowler

about / work / blog

I’m Halle Fowler, a twenty-two-year-old filmmaker and digital artist, writing and creating my way through the headaches and heartbreaks of being a twenty something with nothing better to do. This blog has been going since 2015 and has undergone many transformations; from teen angst fuelled Adults-Don’t-Understand to I’m Twenty and I Understand the World. The iteration you see today is a young lady fucking up, freaking out and turning it into art. My work is always inspired by the place I’m living, which is Sydney for now, and the cool people I meet through the projects I’m on. Check out my previous work and stay tuned on my blog for a steady stream of embarrassing stories and the occasional strokes of genius they inspire. Halle
