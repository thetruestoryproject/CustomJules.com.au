3D Printed Race Buggy

Race Buggy

This is a Model that was Designed by 3DSets. It takes about 15 days of continuous printing to compleate and is one of the most advanced models I have made including tight tolerances and a finly tuned michene.
There was alot of trouble shooting involved, as you need to find matching hardwear that would work with the printed file.
The paint job and assesories were done in house. Also includes flexable TPU tires
